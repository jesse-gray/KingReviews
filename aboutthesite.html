About the Site

This website was created with the sole purpose of reviewing movies, in the hopes that it can inform and educate other movie lovers about the movies I have seen. I understand that not everyone will agree with my viewpoints, as everyone has different tastes in movies, genres, actors/actresses and even directors. It is my hope however, that this site is a guide for people with similar tastes to mine.

I try to be as open minded as i can with my reviews, and to include a diverse assortment of genres and styles to reach a larger audience and to help more people be informed of my view on movies. So if you think you like the same sort of movies as I do, enjoy the website, leave me some feedback, or plet me know any requests for reviews you think I should look into. Again, I will endeavour to do my best to watch and review as much as I can from as many varying genres, directors and film styles as I can.
