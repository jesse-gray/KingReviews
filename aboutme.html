About Me

Entertainee, movie lover, irrational critic. These are terms I use to describe myself, and are part of the reason I decided to create this site. It simply wasn't enough to just watch movies, I had to judge them, and not only judge them, but to tell everyone how I judged them. It is fuundamentaly a character flaw, but it is my hopes that this site can turn it into a strength, or at the very least, help others to find good movies to watch.

I have always been an avid movie watcher, and it continues today. Watching a wide variety of movies and genres allows me to get perspective on the quality of movies as a whole (at least in ym eyes). I aim to empart some of this perspective on to you, fellow moviegoers, in the form of quick, succinct movie reviews and scores, with a little bit of my personality thrown in as well.
